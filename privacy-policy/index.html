Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026
This app is designed for children under 16. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our educational service and we never use children's data for advertising. Parental or guardian consent is required before a child account is created.

EcoleUP ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you or your child uses the EcoleUP mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you confirm that you have read and understood this policy. If you are a parent or legal guardian and you do not agree with this policy, please do not allow your child to use the Service.

1. Who This Policy Applies To

EcoleUP is an educational platform intended for learners, including children under the age of 16. We recognize that children are entitled to special privacy protections under applicable law, including:

  • The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States
  • The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and its provisions for minors in the European Union
  • Applicable local data protection laws in other jurisdictions

2. Parental Consent

We require verifiable parental or guardian consent before we knowingly collect any personal data from a child under the age of 16. The consent process is completed within the app during account registration. A parent or guardian must:

  • Review and accept this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service
  • Provide their own contact information (email address)
  • Approve the child's account creation

If we discover that personal data has been collected from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that data promptly. To report such a concern, contact us at ecoleup2@gmail.com.

3. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our educational service:

Account Information

  • Parent or guardian email address (used for consent and account management)
  • Child's first name or chosen display name
  • Child's age range (to tailor content appropriately)
  • Password (stored as a one-way hash; we never store the plaintext)

Learning Activity Data

  • Lessons completed, exercises attempted, and scores achieved
  • Time spent on each learning module
  • Progress milestones and achievement badges earned

Device and Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system version (iOS or Android)
  • App version
  • Crash logs and error reports (collected anonymously)
  • General geographic region (country-level only, derived from app store or language settings)

We do not collect:

  • Full name, home address, phone number, or precise location data
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Social media profiles or contact lists
  • Payment details (purchases are processed by the app store; we do not receive card data)

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Create and manage the child's learning account
  • Deliver personalized educational content and track progress
  • Send parents or guardians important service updates and progress reports
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app stability
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use children's data for behavioral advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose unrelated to education.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party providers who assist in operating the Service (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) under strict data processing agreements that prohibit them from using data for any other purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: When required to comply with applicable law, regulation, court order, or governmental request.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of EcoleUP, our users, or the public.

Any third-party service providers we use for features within the children's experience comply with COPPA and are prohibited from collecting children's data for their own purposes.

6. Advertising

EcoleUP does not display targeted or behavioral advertisements to children. If any contextual or non-personalized advertising is shown to adult account holders, it is clearly separated from the child experience and based only on non-personal, contextual signals.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as the account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When an account is deleted:

  • Personal identifiers are deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated learning statistics may be retained for product improvement
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal data
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting children's personal data, we will notify affected parents or guardians and relevant authorities as required by law.

9. Parental Rights

As a parent or legal guardian, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about your child
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your child's account and all associated personal data
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke your consent at any time, which will result in account deactivation
  • Object: Object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ecoleup2@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before fulfilling the request.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where this occurs, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place (such as standard contractual clauses) consistent with applicable data protection law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify parents or guardians of material changes by email or in-app notice at least 30 days before they take effect. The revised policy will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: ecoleup2@gmail.com

For COPPA-related inquiries specifically, you may also contact the U.S. Federal Trade Commission at www.ftc.gov.
